# awesome-ancient-game

A curated list of high-quality resources, tools, and websites for learning, playing, researching, and sharing ancient games with the world.

### Learn and Play Ancient Games

* [British Museum: Top 10 Historical Board Games](https://www.britishmuseum.org/blog/top-10-historical-board-games) - A trusted museum guide to ancient and historical games such as the Royal Game of Ur, senet, wari, pachisi, and mehen.
* [RoyalUr.net](https://royalur.net/) - A simple online website for playing the Royal Game of Ur and helping people experience one of the world’s oldest playable board games.
* [The Online Guide to Traditional Games](https://www.tradgames.org.uk/) - A detailed educational resource covering the history and rules of traditional games from many cultures.
* [Ludii Portal](https://ludii.games/) - A powerful platform for playing, studying, and modelling traditional strategy games from around the world.
* [Masters Traditional Games: Ancient & Historical Board Games](https://www.mastersofgames.com/cat/board/board-games-historical.htm) - A useful catalogue for discovering physical replicas of ancient games such as senet, mehen, hnefatafl, and the Royal Game of Ur.

### Research and Preservation

* [British Museum: Royal Game of Ur Collection Object](https://www.britishmuseum.org/collection/object/W_1928-1009-378) - A primary museum collection page for studying the original Royal Game of Ur artefact.
* [Board Game Studies Journal](https://sciendo.com/journal/BGS) - An academic journal focused on historical and systematic research into board games across cultures.
* [Analog Game Studies](https://analoggamestudies.org/) - An open-access publication useful for broader research on tabletop, board, and non-digital game culture.
* [Modern Techniques for Ancient Games](https://arxiv.org/abs/2101.10066) - A research paper explaining how computational methods can help reconstruct and understand ancient games.
* [Foundations of Digital Archæoludology](https://arxiv.org/abs/1905.13516) - A foundational research paper on using digital tools to analyse and reconstruct ancient and traditional games.
